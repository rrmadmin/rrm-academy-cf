## Where the backlink growth came from

Backlink count jumped 600 in 12 days. The bulk is **one domain** — `ifidir.com` — which alone contributed 687 dofollow links from a single spam farm (first_seen 2026-04-12, is_spam=true, DR=27, 0 traffic). Now disavowed.

Real refdomain growth (+31) split across legitimate podcast/RSS aggregators (poddtoppen.se, getpodcast.com, podscan.fm), Substack newsletters, and a handful of organic editorial mentions.
