## What's working

1. **Commentary spotlight format**: `/commentary/rrm-spotlight-naomi-whittaker-md/` ranks #1 for "naomi whittaker md". The patrick-yeung spotlight ranks #9 for "patrick yeung". Spotlight pages are a repeatable authority-building pattern.
2. **Long-form research commentary**: the isthmocele commentary holds position 9 for "isthmocele" (1,500 vol). Long-tail clinical terms convert.
3. **Pillar pages indexing**: `/what-is-rrm/`, `/naprotechnology/`, `/femm/`, `/glossary/*` all rank for their target terms — just not high enough yet.
4. **Podcast distribution**: 7 buzzsprout links, plus rss.com, pocketcasts, poddtoppen — Naomi's podcast appearances are reliably indexed.

## What needs work

1. **Disavow the spam clusters.** ~30+ refdomains are pure link spam. They're inflating the count without contributing authority.
2. **Push glossary terms onto page 1.** `fulguration` (4,400 vol, pos 52), `marquette method` (1,400 vol, pos 30), `vasovasostomy` (1,300 vol, pos 48) — high-volume terms one or two refresh cycles away from page 1 if expanded with deeper content + better internal links.
3. **`naprotechnology` consolidation.** Two pages competing: `/naprotechnology/` pillar (pos 17) and `/glossary/naprotechnology-definition/` (pos 47). Likely cannibalizing — confirm canonical and consider 301'ing the glossary entry to the pillar with anchor-fragment.
4. **DR plateau at 28.** Need 2-3 more high-DR editorial mentions to push past 30. Current legitimate inflow is ~2 quality links per 40 days — not enough velocity. Outreach surface: pitch the spotlight format to other clinicians' substack/podcasts where Naomi has appeared.
