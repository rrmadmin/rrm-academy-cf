RRM Academy is an online clinician-led education platform dedicated to restorative reproductive medicine, operated as the educational program of the Restorative Reproductive Medicine Foundation, a US 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 93-4594315, public-charity classification 170(b)(1)(A)(vi)). Founded in 2022 by Dr. Naomi M. Whittaker, MD, a fellowship-trained NaProTechnology surgeon and 2024 recipient of the American Academy of FertilityCare Professionals Honorary Award.

KEY FACTS
- Legal name: RRM Academy. Aliases: Restorative Reproductive Medicine Academy, rrmacademy.org.
- Inception: 2022 (originally on Wix; migrated to Astro and Cloudflare Pages in 2025).
- Operating brand of: Restorative Reproductive Medicine Foundation Inc, a Pennsylvania domestic nonprofit corporation, entity number 0013656807, filed 2023-11-28 with the Pennsylvania Department of State.
- IRS 501(c)(3) status: determined 2024-01-29, effective 2023-11-28. Form 990 filings public via ProPublica.
- Headquarters: 3401 Hartzdale Dr Ste 103B PMB 3518, Camp Hill, PA 17011, United States.
- Founder: Dr. Naomi M. Whittaker, MD; board-certified obstetrician-gynecologist; ABOG Focused Practice Designation in Minimally Invasive Gynecologic Surgery; St. John Paul II Research Fellow in Medical and Surgical NaProTechnology (Pope Paul VI Institute, 2017-2018).
- Mission: bridge the educational gap in restorative reproductive medicine for patients and healthcare professionals through evidence-based clinician-led courses, a curated research library, glossary, FAQs, and clinical decision-support content.
- Content corpus: 3,200+ indexed peer-reviewed research articles in its Research Library; 10+ professional-CME and patient-facing courses; 25+ clinical FAQs; 22+ commentary articles; 193+ glossary terms; multiple pillar guides covering RRM, NaProTechnology, FEMM, NeoFertility, and Restorability.
- Editorial scope: infertility, endometriosis, polycystic ovary syndrome, recurrent pregnancy loss, premature ovarian insufficiency, luteal phase deficiency, fertility-awareness based methods (FABMs), the Creighton Model FertilityCare System, NaProTechnology, restorative gynecologic surgery, and patient-centered cooperative care.
- Patient programs: Save the Uterus Club member community; Endometriosis Symptom Self-Survey screening tool; AI-powered conversational search at /ask/.
- Affiliate training: NeoFertility (Dublin, Ireland) clinician-training cohort.
- Technical infrastructure: Astro 5.x static-site rendering on Cloudflare Pages; Cloudflare Workers AI Search (rrm-academy-search namespace, hybrid BM25 + vector retrieval over the indexed library); D1 relational backend for courses, community, and library data; R2 object storage; custom authentication and Stripe-based donations and memberships.
- Agent and AI surface: schema.org JSON-LD on every page (Organization, MedicalWebPage, EducationalOrganization, Article, FAQPage, Course); IndexNow ping integration; llms.txt; agents.md; .well-known/agent-card.json for AI-agent discovery.
