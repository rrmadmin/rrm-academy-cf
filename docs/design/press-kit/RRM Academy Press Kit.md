# RRM Academy Press Kit

## About RRM Academy

RRM Academy is the leading educational platform for patients and professionals seeking to understand and apply Restorative Reproductive Medicine (RRM). As the primary educational project of the Restorative Reproductive Medicine Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit, our focus is entirely on education for true healing. Guided by the expertise of our founder, Dr. Naomi Whittaker, and a community of RRM-trained professionals, we provide the evidence-based tools to find the root cause of health issues and restore the body to its natural, healthy function. Our educational resources are designed to meet people exactly where they are on their health journey. Today, RRM Academy is a global community of patients and professionals committed to a better standard of care. Our curriculum, guided by Dr. Whittaker and taught by a faculty of RRM-trained experts, is designed to empower with knowledge and equip with the tools for authentic, restorative healing.

## Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide expert-led, evidence-based education that empowers both patients and healthcare professionals to pursue a new model of care–one that seeks to find and treat the root cause of health issues.

## What is Restorative Reproductive Medicine (RRM)?

Restorative Reproductive Medicine (RRM) is an approach within conventional medicine that works cooperatively with the body. It uses standard and advanced diagnostic tools to investigate the fundamental "why" behind symptoms. By identifying and treating the underlying cause–be it hormonal, anatomical, or inflammatory–we can restore natural function and optimize health. While our work is primarily focused on women, the RRM model also provides powerful solutions for men seeking to improve their reproductive health. This is not about symptom suppression; it's about authentic healing.

### The RRM Approach

The RRM approach is a progressive process that starts with listening. The educational philosophy is built on a simple idea: you deserve to understand your body. The core principles that guide the curriculum for both patients and professionals are:

1.  **Identify the cause:** Your body's signals... the painful periods, the irregular cycles-are the clues that have likely been ignored. We teach you how to chart your cycle, turning your experiences into valuable data. For a NaPro-trained OB/GYN, that chart is like an EKG for a cardiologist; it helps uncover the root cause of your health issues.
2.  **Treat the condition:** Once we know the why, we can discover targeted medical and surgical treatments that address the underlying disease. If you have endometriosis, the goal isn't just to manage the pain; it's to skillfully resect the disease itself, like carefully removing weeds from a garden so the flowers can grow.
3.  **Restore normal function:** The ultimate goal is to restore your body's natural hormonal balance and reproductive function. A healthy system is a fertile system. This approach can lead to improved fertility and healthier pregnancies because we've fixed the broken pipe instead of just using buckets to catch the leak.
4.  **Cooperate with the body:** RRM works in harmony with your natural cycle. You will learn to understand and appreciate your body's intricate design, becoming an active, empowered partner in your own healthcare journey. We're not fighting your body; we're working with it.

## Programs and Services

RRM Academy bridges the gap between complex medical science and the people who need it most. We do this by:

*   **Offering Clinician-Led Courses:** Our courses are developed and taught by medical experts, translating years of clinical experience into accessible online education. Our self-paced online courses include video lectures, downloadable resources, and transcripts, allowing you to learn on your own schedule.
*   **Empowering Patients:** We equip individuals and couples with the knowledge to understand their bodies, ask informed questions, and advocate for better care.
*   **Training Professionals:** We provide resources for healthcare providers who want to integrate restorative principles into their own practices.
*   **Sharing Evidence-Based Resources:** From in-depth masterclasses to our extensive research library, all of our content is grounded in scientific evidence and clinical best practices.

## Leadership

**Naomi Whittaker, MD**
*Board Chair & Chief Medical Director*

Dr. Naomi Whittaker is the Board Chair and Chief Medical Director of the Restorative Reproductive Medicine Foundation. She is a board-certified OB/GYN with specialized fellowship training in Medical and Surgical NaProTECHNOLOGY, a specific and highly effective type of RRM. Dr. Whittaker is a passionate advocate for women's health and an experienced minimally invasive surgeon. She founded RRM Academy out of a conviction that every woman has the right to understand her body and receive care that respects her dignity and aims for true restoration. Her mission is to bring this hope and healing to as many people as possible.

**Brian Whittaker**
*Board Treasurer & Acting Executive Lead*

Brian Whittaker is the Board Treasurer and Acting Executive Lead of the Restorative Reproductive Medicine Foundation. He manages the overall operations of the foundation, implements strategy, and oversees RRM Academy, including the development of RRM Surgery University (RRM Surgery U). He also manages freelancers, and drives fundraising and growth for the organization.
