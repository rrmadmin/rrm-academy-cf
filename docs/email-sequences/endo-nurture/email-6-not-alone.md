## Body

Hi [First Name],

I had endometriosis symptoms for over 20 years before my diagnosis. I was already four and a half years into my OBGYN training.

I understood the disease. I knew the research. And I still spent two decades without a name for what was happening in my own body.

I built this survey, the Prep Kit, and the Masterclass in Endometriosis and Surgery because no one should have to piece together what I learned the hard way. The information exists. It just is not in the places patients are told to look.

You have been in this sequence for three weeks. You have read about diagnostic delay, surgical workup, and questions to ask a surgeon. You are not the person you were when you opened that survey.

When you are ready to evaluate your surgical options, the Masterclass in Endometriosis and Surgery is there. It exists for the moment when you need it. Not before.

We are also building a directory of RRM clinicians and NaPro-trained surgeons. When it goes live, you will be among the first to know.

One more thing: if you are a clinician reading this, this course was built for both patients and providers. Several clinicians who completed it said it taught them what residency did not.

From here, I will continue sharing research and clinical updates through our regular emails. You are part of this community now.

You do not have to figure this out alone.

Dr. Naomi Whittaker, MD, Board-Certified OBGYN, MIGS, NFPMC, FCI
