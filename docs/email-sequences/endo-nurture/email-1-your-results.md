## Body

Hi [First Name],

Your results PDF is attached. If you missed the download on the results page, here is the link again: [PDF LINK]

Save it to your phone now, before this email gets buried. You will want it at your appointment.

Before that visit, three things worth doing:

1. Open the PDF and circle the symptoms that concern you most. Not all of them. The ones that feel urgent or that you keep dismissing.
2. Note the ones you have never mentioned to a doctor. Those matter most.
3. Write down one question you want answered at your next visit. One. Not a list yet.

The goal is to walk in prepared. Doctors move fast. When you come in with a marked-up document and a specific question, the conversation changes.

One thing worth knowing about me: I am a board-certified OBGYN who completed a fellowship in NaProTechnology, and I spent over 20 years watching women walk out of appointments with no answers despite clear clinical patterns.

This survey does not diagnose anything. But it gives you language. And language changes what happens in that room.

Dr. Naomi Whittaker, MD, Board-Certified OBGYN, MIGS, NFPMC, FCI
