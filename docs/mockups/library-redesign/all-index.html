How to choose. The four mockups trade off density, discoverability, and editorial voice. A (Dense list) is the right pick for clinical users who skim everything on every visit — closest to "PubMed alerts in HTML." B (Card grid) is the safest choice for visual continuity with the new homepage. C (Sidebar filters) is the right pick if facet-search is the primary browse motion (topic + year + journal + access). D (Magazine) is most ambitious — gives a clear "front of the issue" signal but only works if there's always a legitimately spotlight-worthy paper to feature.
