## Methodology

1. 8 primary seeds from the brief + 22 topical expansion seeds (cost, conditions, providers, effectiveness, criticism, male factor).
2. Serper.dev `/search` for each seed, parsed `peopleAlsoAsk` (level 1), `relatedSearches`, organic results.
3. Serper.dev `/autocomplete` for the 8 primary seeds.
4. Each level-1 PAA re-queried to surface level-2 PAAs. Dedup by lowercased question text.
5. Manual classification against current page + editorial rules (Catholic framing forbidden, IVF-legit flagged).
6. Clustered into 10 consolidated recommendations with priority ranking.
