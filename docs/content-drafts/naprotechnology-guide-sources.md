## Content Rules

- Success rates: ONLY Boyle 2025 (26-41% crude LBR) and Sanchez-Mendez 2025 (50-62% adjusted cumulative, n=1,310). NOT old Hilgers/PPVI data from ebook.
- IVF comparison: balanced framing from what-is-rrm. "RRM is its own paradigm, not defined in opposition to IVF."
- Letrozole for PCOS: FLAG for Naomi review. Do not assert as NaPro standard.
- Creighton Model: mention alongside NaPro at least once (two halves of one system).
- PCOS treatment: remove "ovarian wedge resection" from ebook.
- Endometriosis: excision, not ablation.
- Each section opens with a direct-answer sentence (citable by AI).
- Inline citations: `<sup><a href="/library/[slug]/">N</a></sup>` for library articles.
- Mark unverified claims with `[VERIFY]`.
- **Cost comparison framing (anchoring):** Present IVF cost anchor first ($40K-$60K), then position RRM as "a fraction of that." Lead with insurance coverage angle. Do NOT use specific multipliers (the "20x" figure from what-is-rrm is hallucinated). Do NOT lead with specific RRM dollar amounts.
