## Critical Blockers

These three issues should be resolved before all other work.

### 1. ~~/ what-is-rrm/ Missing from XML Sitemap~~ FIXED (March 20, 2026)

**Previously:** The page was explicitly excluded in the `astro.config.mjs` sitemap filter (along with `/common-questions-about-rrm/`). Both pillar pages were invisible to sitemap-based crawling.

**Fixed:** Removed both pages from the exclude array. Verified both now appear in `sitemap-0.xml`. Commit `4ad6d18`.

### 2. ~~AI Crawler Robots.txt Rules Too Narrow~~ FIXED (March 20, 2026)

**Previously:** GPTBot, PerplexityBot, and ClaudeBot blocks only allowed `/library/` and `/llms.txt`, blocking AI crawlers from 95% of the site. Per robots.txt spec, specific user-agent blocks completely override the wildcard.

**Fixed:** Robots.txt restructured with three tiers: AI search crawlers (ChatGPT-User, OAI-SearchBot, Claude-Web, Perplexity-User, Applebot-Extended), AI training crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended, Meta-ExternalAgent, Amazonbot, cohere-ai, CCBot), and blocked scrapers (Bytespider). All get `Allow: /` with only private paths blocked. Commit `e11f423`.

### 3. ~~Zero Pillar-to-Pillar Body Content Links~~ FIXED (March 20, 2026)

**Previously:** Zero cross-links between pillar pages in body content.

**Fixed:** 16 cross-links added across all 5 pillar guides. what-is-rrm links to NaPro, FEMM, NeoFertility, glossary. naprotechnology links to what-is-rrm, FEMM, NeoFertility. neofertility links to naprotechnology, what-is-rrm. femm links to what-is-rrm, neofertility (already had 2 NaPro links). glossary links all 4 pillar pages from definition headings. Commit `af88281`.
