## What RRM is

Painful periods are not a personality trait. Irregular cycles are not "just how you are." Infertility is not unexplained — it is undiagnosed. Restorative Reproductive Medicine is the clinical discipline that diagnoses and treats the underlying causes of reproductive dysfunction rather than bypassing or suppressing them. The goal is not indefinite condition management. The goal is answers and restored function.

A woman's cycle is diagnostic data, not background noise.

## Scope and editorial position

RRM Academy educates on the full scope of reproductive health: endometriosis, PCOS, menstrual disorders, recurrent miscarriage, hormonal imbalance, and infertility. Content should not be used to recommend IVF, to frame hormonal suppression as curative treatment, or to frame RRM as solely a fertility intervention.

NaProTechnology and the Creighton Model System are distinct methodologies with their own histories and clinical communities. They share common principles with RRM but are not subsets of it.
