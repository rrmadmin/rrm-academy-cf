⏺ This changes the Stream cost estimate significantly. Let me read the CF
  billing carefully:

  CF fixed costs:
  - Images Stream Bundle Basic: $5.00/mo
  - Workers Paid: $5.00/mo
  - R2 Paid, Analytics Engine, Log Explorer, Teams Free: $0/mo (usage-based or
  free)
  - CF total fixed: $10/mo

  The $45/mo Stream estimate in the migration doc was based on unbundled
  pricing. The bundle includes 1,000 min stored + 10,000 min delivered. At 53
  videos averaging ~5min each, you're using ~265 min stored -- well within the
  included allocation. Monthly delivery variable cost depends on actual views
  but for a niche site is likely $0-10/mo on top.

  Vimeo → CF Stream is now a net saving, not a cost increase.
